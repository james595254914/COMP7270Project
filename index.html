The Harm Of Smoking

Lung Cancer

Medical clinical studies have confirmed that smoking is the main cause of lung cancer. Statistics show that 90% of people who die of lung cancer are smokers.

Heart Disease

The nicotine and carbon monoxide in cigarettes affect heart function, and smokers are more likely to suffer from heart disease than people who do not smoke.

Other Diseases

Smoking also greatly increases the risk of various diseases, such as throat cancer, oral cancer, gastric ulcer,emphysema and pneumonia and so on.

Shortens Life

Medical research has proved that the average life expectancy of smokers is three years shorter than that of non-smokers.In order to save life, please quit smoiking!

Reason for starting to smoking



According to the data, most of people who starting to smoking was influenced by friends. In addition, A large proportion of people who smoke because of curiosity and social needs A small number of people smoke for other reasons, such as being influenced by relatives, or just want to kill time, and other reasons(like Refreshing one’s mind,Looking more mature / stylish or Influenced by public figures ).

Reason for giving up smoking

32.8% of ex-daily cigarette smokers gave up smoking on their own accord because health had already been harmed by smoking

28.9% gave up smoking because health had not yet been harmed by smoking, but wanted to prevent it from being harmed.

Some 208 800 (32.7%) daily smokers had tried but failed to give up smoking.
